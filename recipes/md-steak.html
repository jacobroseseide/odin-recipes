Matt Damon Steak

Description:

In the first image, actor Matt Damon is seen sharing food with a chef, both enjoying the culinary experience. The second image showcases a delicious plate of beef satay skewers, garnished with fresh herbs and served with a side of peanut sauce, highlighting the intricate and appetizing presentation of the dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp cracked black peppercorns
  • Kosher salt
  • 1/4 cup red wine
  • 6 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3/4 tsp chopped fresh thyme or 1/4 tsp dried thyme leaves
  • 2 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1/2 cup plus 2 tbsp canola oil
  • 1 lb hanger steak, trimmed and sliced across grain into 1/4-inch-thick strips
  • 20 (6- to 8-inch) bamboo skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Stir peppercorns in a small skillet over medium-high heat for 2 to 4 minutes until fragrant and smoking.
  2. Transfer to a small bowl. Add 1 tsp salt and next 5 ingredients. Whisk until gradually into mixture until smooth.
  3. Toss beef in oil mixture to coat. Cover and chill for 1 hour.
  4. Thread beef strips onto skewers, leaving 1 to 2 inches open at one end. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Preheat grill to medium-high. Cook beef to desired doneness, about 2 to 3 minutes per side.

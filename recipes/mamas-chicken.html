Special chicken from Mama

Description

"Mama's Chicken" is a dish that embodies both beauty and tradition, with its rich, mouthwatering appearance and perfectly grilled drumsticks coated in a glossy, caramelized barbecue sauce. The deep, golden-brown hue of the chicken, contrasted with the vibrant red of the sauce, creates an inviting visual appeal that promises a delicious and satisfying meal. Traditionally, "Mama's Chicken" is more than just a meal; it's a culinary experience rooted in family gatherings and cherished recipes passed down through generations, often involving a secret marinade or spice blend lovingly crafted by mothers and grandmothers, and typically cooked over a grill or open flame to evoke memories of backyard barbecues and communal feasts.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 large cloves garlic, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 10 chicken drumsticks
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • ½ cup finely chopped onion
  • ¾ cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce

Steps

  1. Mash brown sugar, garlic, salt, and pepper together to form a paste. Coat chicken with paste in a resealable bag and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour.
  2. Heat oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Sauté onion for 5 minutes, then stir in ketchup, vinegar, and Worcestershire sauce. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate. Remove chicken from the bag.
  4. Grill chicken for 3 to 5 minutes per side. Move to indirect heat and cook at 300 to 350 degrees F for 20 minutes, turning halfway. Baste with sauce and cook, basting every 10 minutes, for another 20 to 25 minutes until internal temperature reaches 165 degrees F.
